Infolab Project-Baby Name Lab

Brief introduction: An interactive product that enabling parents to have a customizable list of names for their newborn baby and narrow down name choices.Problem: For parents, finding the perfect name for their baby can be a sweet burden which means pressure, simply because there are too many options of baby names. Solution: With Baby Bracket, parents are able to have more inspirations on their baby name choices with the ability to customize a baby name list and interactive way to narrow down the list until reaching the idea name that already in their heart.
