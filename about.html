Chunying was born and raised in China. She obtained her bachelor and master degree of law in Beihang University and University College London, after which she worked as a legal counsel in the largest Trust fund of China, Citic Trust Co.. During her participation in a company project related to trust law legal research, she helped design the legal research website where she dug her passion regarding UX design. She is now a first-year graduate of MSI program at University of Michigan with a focus on UX Design and wishes to bring more technology and aesthetic sides of her.
